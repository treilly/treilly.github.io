Tech Executive Leadership Institute (TELI) desk research tool

What is this?

A tool to assist with desk research of policy initiatives. Basically it's a version of Google focused on a subset of the web relevant to policy research (Federal websites, News, Non-profits and think tanks)

What can I search?

Currently there are several categories of sites:

  • Press - cnbc.com, cnn.com, WaPo, NYTimes, Twitter, Politico, C-Span
  • Non-profits - Non-profits and think tanks with quite a bit of pre-existing policy research
  • Non-profits tech resources - Non-profits focused on technology
  • Federal government - All encompassing
  • Congress - senate.gov and house.gov
  • Federal legislation - The federal register of all passed and proposed legislation
  • Federal tech resources - USDS.gov and 18-F
  • Executive branch - whitehouse.gov
  • Federal agencies - DOL, CBO, FEMA, VA, and many others
  • Judiciary - uscourts.gov and JDSupra.com

How does it work?

This tool uses a Google feature called a Programmable Search Engine to allow customization of Google's search engine
